Physical Health

Physical health is about a collection of habits that make up who you are and the health of your body. Physical health is affected by levels of activity (exercise), what you eat (nutrition), environmental factors such as toxins in your environment, and even mental habits affect your physical health!

Exercise

Exercise is hugely important to physical and mental health. No matter how well you eat, without including exercise in your routine consistently, you won't be your optimal health.
